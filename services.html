What Waylight helps with

Sort the business side before growth turns into clutter.

This is for small businesses that are doing fine on the outside but messy behind the scenes. Waylight Atlantic helps tidy up the day-to-day systems around files, folders, permissions, templates, and handover so a growing team can work without constant guesswork.

Review

Find the friction that keeps slowing the week down

Where do files go missing? Who has access? Which version is current? What happens when someone new starts or someone is off? Waylight starts with the places a small owner or manager already feels the strain.

Rebuild

Put simple rules in place that people will actually follow

The aim is not a heavy manual. It is a working setup: clearer folders, straightforward names, sensible access, and a short handover guide that suits a team of one to nine people.

What gets put in place

Shared structure A folder and records setup that matches how the business actually works.
File rules Simple naming and version habits so people stop saving over each other or guessing which file matters.
Access and ownership Clear decisions about who can see, edit, and keep key information tidy.
Handover guide Short notes so the business is not relying on one person's memory every time something changes.
